Design System · v1.0

Iron & Grit

Visual language for the kettlebell workout timer. A dark, high-contrast aesthetic built on two typefaces, a single brand accent, and deliberate use of glow and shadow to convey intensity.

Mode Dark only
Primary font Barlow

Base unit 4px

Motion
