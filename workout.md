** Workout A **

This is a strength focussed workout.

Repeat the following 3 times in total:

|Excercise|Reps|Weight|Rest in seconds|
|---|---|---|---|
|Squats|12|16kg|60|
|Clean & Press|12|12kg|60|
|Kettlebell Rows|15|20kg|90|

** Workout B **

This is a conditioning focussed workout.

Repeat the following 6 times in total using the EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute):

|Excercise|Reps|Weight|
|---|---|---|
|Kettlebell Swings|20|12kg|
|Kettlebell Thrusters|8|16kg|
